# QUINNHAVEN - ELEMENTOR DESIGN & CONTENT BLUEPRINT

Use this blueprint to build out your Homepage in Elementor natively. Since you want to be able to "click and edit" text and images visually, you will need to drag and drop Elementor's native widgets (Heading, Text Editor, Image, Button) and simply copy/paste the content below.

---

## 🎨 GLOBAL BRAND IDENTITY
Set these in your Elementor "Site Settings" (the hamburger menu in the top left of Elementor > Site Settings > Global Colors & Global Fonts).

**Global Colors:**
* Primary (Brand Blue): `#141162`
* Accent (Gold): `#B8995E`
* Accent Light (Gold Light): `#d4b881`
* Text/Dark (Charcoal): `#1a1a1a`
* Background (Cream): `#f5f2ed`

**Global Fonts:**
* Primary Heading Font: `Playfair Display` (Serif)
* Body Text Font: `Inter` (Sans-serif)
